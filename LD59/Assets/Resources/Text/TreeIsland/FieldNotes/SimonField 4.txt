Aug 5, 1989: Simon Griggs 
Genus: Phaseolus vulgaris
Family: Fabaceae

A control set of seeds has been set in an atmospheric pressure chamber of 1153.82 hPa. There has been none of the expected development in the seed and germination is fully halted. More field data needs to be sampled at the quarry to determine the growth rate of domestic plants in the area.
