The sun is not setting! I was down below deck and feeling queasy so I brewed up some tea and headed up on deck to see the sunset, it usually helps. After a while Simon joined me and we talked a bit about his farm back home and all the plants he wanted to bring back with him from the islands, and the hours went by but still the sun was where it was at the start of our conversation, overhead staring down on our heads. I don’t know what to make of this, but Simon quickly ran down to his bunk, I think he said something about acorns.
