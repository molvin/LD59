It is amazing the things the Valders were able to do with their primitive technology, I have seen constructions here that rival the Sumerians during the late third millennia and that is out here on these small islands. Carol has started working on translating some of the old scripts but so far most of what we have gotten is old transactional records and star tracking charts. If we just manage to get in to that golden door perhaps we might be able to know more of how they were able to sustain a life out here.
